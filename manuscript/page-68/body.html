Various Kinds Of Ecraseurs.

Linear Crushing.

The originator of the use of that peculiar instrument, the ecraseur (), so valuable an adjunct in the operation of castration, is Mr. H. Bouley, who brought it into use at a date as early as the year 1857. It is not, therefore, an American invention, as has sometimes been claimed.
