Abscesses.

When these are likely to result from a too rapid closure of the edges of the scrotal envelope, the premature union may be readily prevented, as we have before stated, by the careful introduction of the finger into the wound while it is still suppurating. But notwithstanding this precaution they will sometimes occur as the result of the infiltration and accumulation of the suppurative matter.
